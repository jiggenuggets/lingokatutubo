About This Project

Preserving and revitalizing Bagobo-Tagabawa through practical document tools.

Mission

LingoKatutubo supports language preservation by making translated educational and community documents easier to produce, inspect, and archive.

The system combines document extraction, OCR, a local multilingual phrasebook, and layout-aware PDF reconstruction so translated output can remain close to the source document.

Bagobo-Tagabawa Context

The Bagobo-Tagabawa people are an indigenous community of Mindanao with rich artistic and linguistic traditions. This project treats translation as a reviewable preservation workflow, not as a replacement for community expertise.

How It Works

1Upload and identify the document type.
2Extract text, page geometry, and OCR blocks when needed.
3Translate with the local phrasebook cascade.
4Reconstruct, preview, and download the translated PDF.
